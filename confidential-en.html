Why Do We Use the Microphone?

The Binaural Sounds Generator app uses microphone access (RECORD_AUDIO) to allow you to record ambient noises from your environment. This feature helps generate a personalized white noise tailored to your relaxation or focus needs.

How it works: During a 2-second recording, captured sounds are analyzed locally to determine a dominant frequency. This frequency is then used to create a sound that effectively masks surrounding noises. No data is stored, transmitted, or shared with third parties.

Your control: You can deny this permission in your device settings at any time. If you choose not to grant it, the app will continue to function with predefined frequencies.

For more information, see our Privacy Policy.
